Teaching


I am an Experienced Guitar Teacher and Gigging Musiciain offering One-to-One Lessons in the Chorlton/Whalley Range area. I teach Rock, Pop, Jazz, Blues, Music Theory and Production. I also teach both Acoustic and Electric Guitar.


    RNCM Graduate,
    9+ Years Teaching Experience,
    Specialise in Teaching Beginners/Intermediates of all ages, teaching genres + loops/effects,

I am passionate about improving a students understanding and knowledge of music as a whole, always starting from whatever music the student loves, becoming comfortable with that, and then pushing into new territory, all a bit at a time. I also specialize in teaching students the basics of specific genres and use of effects/loop pedal setups. In my years teaching I have taught Adults who have always wanted to play, prodigiously talented youngsters, friends who all play in a band together and everyone in-between. I always tailor the lesson to what fits the student best. Please get in contact to organise a taster session and for rates. At the moment all lessons are streamed via Zoom.
